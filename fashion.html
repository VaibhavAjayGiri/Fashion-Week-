Elsa Hosk

Hosk was born on 7 November 1988[11] in Stockholm[12]. Growing up in Sweden, Hosk was presented with offers to model during high school after her father submitted photographs to various modeling agencies in Sweden when she was 13;[5] she began modeling at 14.[4] She did some modeling in high school but decided to primarily concentrate on her studies instead (she did some notable work with After playing professional basketball for two years, she began receiving many job offers and soon moved to New York City to begin modeling full-time.
